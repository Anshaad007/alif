Courses

Discover a world of learning at Alif Moral School!
Our courses are designed to nurture every aspect of your child's growth:
• Hifz Course: Embark on a spiritual journey with expert Qur'an memorization guidance.
• Islamic Studies & Quranic Education: Deepen understanding of faith, values, and the Holy Book.
• Arabic Language: Unlock the beauty of Arabic for both daily life and religious study.
• Khutbah Training: Build confidence and leadership through public speaking and sermon delivery.
• Madrassa Classes: Experience a blend of tradition and modernity in a caring, interactive environment.
Join us to inspire, empower, and shape a bright future for your child!
