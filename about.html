🕌 About Us – Alif Online Madrassa

Alif Online Madrassa is a faith-based educational platform committed to nurturing the moral, spiritual, and intellectual development of students across the globe. Rooted in Islamic values and guided by prophetic teachings, our school offers a balanced blend of Qur’anic education, character development, and essential academic learning — all delivered online with care and excellence.

Our mission is to build a generation of confident Muslims who embody Islamic principles in every aspect of life — from daily conduct to critical thinking, from prayer to problem-solving.

🌟 Our Vision

"To nurture hearts with Imaan, minds with knowledge, and lives with purpose — in service to Allah and society."

🎯 What We Offer

  • 📘 Qur’anic Studies with tajweed and tafsir
  • 🕋 Islamic Etiquette & Values for character-building
  • 🧮 Academic Support in English, Math, and Science
  • 🗣️ Arabic Language for understanding the Qur’an
  • 🧑‍🏫 Flexible Batches for children, youth, professionals, and elders

We offer customized learning tracks including Hifdh programs, Tajweed batches, weekend-only classes, and special schemes for Gulf, European, and preschool time zones.
